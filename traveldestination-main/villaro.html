The intimate veranda with the spa tub are the finishing touches to an utterly indulgent holiday. Created with romance and relaxation in mind, Villa Omikron, an oasis of calmness, combines stunning panoramic views with comfort, custom-made furniture and elegant decor giving a luxurious feel to your getaway. As you enter you are greeted by a spacious living room with a dining area that leads you to the king size bedroom which is discreetly secluded. A fully equipped kitchen comes on your right with a discreet arch opening. As you walk deeper into the cave the black Vasaltis marble freestanding bathtub, a generous walk in shower and the double sink relish the serene feel of this gracious suite. The highlight however, is the serene veranda that features a spa tub, which appears to merge with the Caldera a thousand feet below and offers generously magnificent views of the deep blue Aegean Sea.
